## 请求方法
```shell
curl -X POST 'https://api.trongrid.io/jsonrpc' --data '{
    "jsonrpc": "2.0",
    "method": "eth_getBlockByNumber",
    "params": ["42320000", true],
    "id": 1
}'
```
